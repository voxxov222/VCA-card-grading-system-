02 / PROBLEM
The Problem

Grading built trust. Now it's the thing being faked.

Counterfeit slabs, cracked-open cases, and swapped labels are rising as fast as card values are. Once a slab is opened, there's no reliable way for a buyer three owners down the line to know if what's inside still matches the label on the front.

03 / SOLUTION
The Solution

A slab that can prove it hasn't been touched.

VCA builds three layers of verification directly into the encapsulation — physical, electronic, and digital — so authenticity isn't a claim on a sticker, it's a live check anyone can run with a phone.

01

Cryptographic NFC Seal

An embedded chip performs a fresh cryptographic handshake on every tap — no app required, can't be cloned or replayed.

02

Tamper-Loop Circuit

A continuous trace runs through the case seal. Prying, drilling, or cutting breaks the loop and the chip permanently reports it.

03

Real-Time Ledger

Every scan, grade, and ownership event is logged, giving buyers a verifiable history instead of a static certificate.
